\begin{recipe}{Pumpkin-Walnut Muffins}{12 large muffins}{30 minutes prep, 45 minutes baking}
% From march/april Encompass magazine
Preheat the oven to \tempf{350}.

\Ingredient{\flour{\ap}{4}{3}}
\Ingredient{\fr12 cup brown sugar}
\Ingredient{\fr13 cup white sugar}
\Ingredient{1 tablespoon baking powder}
\Ingredient{\fr32 teaspoon cinnamon}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon salt}
\Ingredient{\fr12 cup chopped lightly toasted walnuts}
\Ingredient{\fr12 cup golden raisins}
Combine in a large mixing bowl, adding the large stuff after the first stuff is combined.

\Ingredient{1 egg}
\Ingredient{\fr13 cup plus 1 tablespoon canola oil}
\Ingredient{\fr13 cup plus 1 tablespoon 2\% milk}
\Ingredient{15 ounces pumpkin}
In a separate bowl, mix the wet ingredients.
Then gradually stir into the dry ingredients. Bake until they pass the toothpick test, about 45 minutes. Let cool for 10 minutes before removing from the tins.


\end{recipe}
