\begin{recipe}{One-pan Baked Chicken, Sausage, and Brussels Sprouts}{4 servings}{20 minutes prep, 30 minutes cooking}
% http://www.nerdswithknives.com/one-pan-chicken-sausage-and-brussels-sprouts/

Position rack in lower third of oven and preheat to 232\0C (450\0F).

\Ingredient{450 grams Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved}
\Ingredient{5 shallots, peeled and quartered}
\Ingredient{1 lemon, thinly sliced}
\Ingredient{30 mL olive oil}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon salt}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon pepper}
Combine in a 12-inch cast iron skillet or rimmed baking sheet.

\Ingredient{3 medium garlic cloves, minced}
\Ingredient{20 milliliters Dijon mustard}
\Ingredient{20 milliliters honey}
\Ingredient{15 milliliters Worchestershire sauce}
\Ingredient{3 teaspoons fresh rosemary}
\Ingredient{15 milliliters olive oil}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon pepper}
\Ingredient{\fr12 teaspoon salt}
\Ingredient{4 full, skin-on chicken thighs}
\Ingredient{4 large Italian sausages cut into 2-inch lengths}
Combine in a small bowl until it forms a paste. Rub paste all over the chicken. Place chicken and sausage atop the Brussels sprouts. Roast until sprouts are browned and the chicken is to temperature, 25-30 minutes. If the chicken is done early it can be removed and set aside until the vegetables are done.


\end{recipe}
