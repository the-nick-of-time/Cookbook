\begin{recipe}[PeachPie]{Peach Pie}{8 servings}{1 hour prep, 50 minutes baking}
% https://simplyhomecooked.com/peach-pie-filling/#recipe
Prepare a pie crust as on \reciperef{PieCrust}.

\Ingredient{5 large peaches, about 7 cups when sliced}
Slice the peaches \fr14 inch thick and set aside.

\Ingredient{\fr12 teaspoon vanilla extract}
\Ingredient{\fr32 tablespoon lemon juice}
\Ingredient{3 tablespoons cornstarch}
\Ingredient{\sugar{1/4}}
\Ingredient{\sugar{1/4}}
\Ingredient{\fr18 teaspoon cinnamon}
\Ingredient{1 cup water}
In a large saucepan, heat to a boil. Stir frequently, ensuring nothing gets stuck to the bottom of the pan. Let thicken for 2--3 minutes.

Add peaches to the pan and simmer for 5--7 minutes, until soft but not yet mushy.

Pour into a glass dish and refrigerate. It should be cooled at least to room temperature before you put it in a pie crust.

\preheat{350} Prepare the 2-crust pie as usual, and bake for 50 minutes or until the edges of the crust are golden brown.

\end{recipe}
