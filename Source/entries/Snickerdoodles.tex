\begin{recipe}{Snickerdoodles}{15 cookies}{10 minutes prep + 60 minutes chilling, 10 minutes baking}
% http://lifemadesimplebakes.com/2015/02/favorite-snickerdoodles/

Preheat the oven to 350\0F, unless you plan on chilling the dough.

\Ingredient{\fr12 cup salted butter}
\Ingredient{\fr12 cup granulated sugar}
\Ingredient{\fr14 cup packed brown sugar}
\Ingredient{\fr32 teaspoon vanilla extract}
Beat together the butter, sugar, and vanilla until the mixture is light and fluffy.

\Ingredient{1 egg}
Add the egg and mix until combined.

\Ingredient{\fr32 cup \ap}
\Ingredient{2 teaspoons cornstarch}
\Ingredient{\fr12 teaspoon baking soda}
\Ingredient{\fr12 teaspoon cream of tartar}
Mix together the dry ingredients and slowly add them to the rest.

\Ingredient{2 tablespoons granulated sugar}
\Ingredient{\fr52 teaspoon ground cinnamon}
Mix the cinnamon and sugar. Make balls of the dough and roll them in the cinnamon sugar. Chill these balls for 1 hour in the fridge, or bake immediately.

\newstep
Bake the cookies for 10 minutes.

\end{recipe}
