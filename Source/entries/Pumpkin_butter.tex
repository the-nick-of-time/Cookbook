\begin{recipe}{Pumpkin Butter}{1 cup}{40 minutes}
% from december 2012 delicious living magazine

\Ingredient{1 cup pumpkin pur\'ee}
\Ingredient{\fr12 cup sugar}
\Ingredient{2 teaspoons maple syrup}
\Ingredient{\fr34 teaspoon cinnamon}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon allspice}
\Ingredient{\fr14 teaspoon nutmeg}
\Ingredient{\fr18 teaspoon cardamom}
\Ingredient{1 teaspoon lemon juice}
\Ingredient{Dash of salt}
Combine all ingredients in a nonaluminum pan over medium heat. Simmer, stirring constantly, until flavors are well blended. Bring to a slight boil for 5 minutes. Pour into 2 hot, sterile \fr12 cup jars, leaving \fr12 inch at the top. Cap with hot, sterile lids. Process ing a boiling bath for 20 minutes. Remove to a towel, then chill after dried.

\end{recipe}
